About Donum

WHAT WE DO

Donum Attire was created in Los Angeles in 2015. Originally designed as a “Brand with a Purpose” by the Barocio Brothers.They noticed that not many brands are mionority ownedand wanted to change that. With an unsual business model of giving 15% of every purchase to a charity and 5% to aid in the fight of climate change to be Carbon Neutral.

IT'S IN OUR DNA

Santiago Barocio beleived that there should be a way to support a minority ran business and give to charity at the same time. Donum's core value is to create a brand that can appeal to everyone whilst donating proceeds to charities and the fight in climate change.
